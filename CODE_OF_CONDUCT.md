# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

De:dobe is liberation technology, built on collaboration and mutual respect. We're committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, education, socioeconomic status
- Nationality, personal appearance, race, religion
- Sexual identity and orientation
- Technology choices or preferences

## Our Standards

**Behaviors that contribute to a positive environment:**

- Being kind, empathetic, and respectful
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them learn
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community and users
- Showing appreciation for contributions, big and small
- Disagreeing constructively and professionally

**Unacceptable behaviors:**

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Discrimination or exclusionary behavior
- Sexual attention or advances of any kind
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Philosophy

We value:

1. **Directness over politeness theater** - Be honest and clear, but always respectful
2. **Ideas over ego** - Focus on making De:dobe better, not being right
3. **Learning over gatekeeping** - Help others grow, don't hoard knowledge
4. **Collaboration over competition** - We're building together
5. **Impact over intentions** - Take responsibility when your actions cause harm

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub repository spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, code comments)
- Official communication channels
- Representing De:dobe in public spaces
- Online and offline project-related events

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **For minor issues:** Address directly with the person if you feel safe doing so
2. **For serious issues:** Report to project maintainers at [contact info - to be added]
3. **For urgent safety concerns:** Contact GitHub Support directly

**All reports will be:**
- Handled confidentially
- Reviewed promptly
- Taken seriously

### Consequences

Project maintainers may take any action deemed appropriate, including:

1. **Warning** - Private or public, explaining the violation
2. **Temporary ban** - Time-limited restriction from project spaces
3. **Permanent ban** - Indefinite removal from the community

**Factors considered:**
- Severity and impact of the behavior
- Pattern of behavior vs. isolated incident
- Response to feedback and willingness to change
- Safety of community members

### Appeals

If you believe enforcement action was unjustified:
- Contact project maintainers with your perspective
- Appeals will be reviewed by multiple maintainers when possible
- Decisions aim to balance individual and community well-being

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying and enforcing standards
- Taking fair, appropriate corrective action
- Removing, editing, or rejecting contributions that violate this Code
- Communicating reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate

Maintainers who don't follow or enforce the Code of Conduct may face temporary or permanent repercussions.
